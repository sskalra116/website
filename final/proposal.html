Project Description

What is the topic of your project?
My topic for the project is A Higher State which is a showcase of my hobby,meditation and an informative website on the same.

Why did you choose this topic?
I chose this topic because I think that meditation is the most valuable and helping thing that i've learned in my life and would like to share with the audience the ways,benefits and science behind it to convey them to practise it and improve their lives.

What background knowledge to you bring to this topic?
I started to practice meditation since when I was in my 11th grade and have doing it since so, i would like to share this hobby of mine.

What is your intended audience?
My intended audience are the people who are beginners to this hobby of mine and would like to jump into depths and discover more about this topic .
